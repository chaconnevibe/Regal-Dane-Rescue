    • Regal Dane Rescue

    We are dedicated to the rescue, rehabilitation and rehoming of abandoned, neglected and owner-surrendered Great Danes of all ages. We also provide education to the public about the Great Dane breed and about responsible pet ownership.

    We Need Your Help!

    After 10 years, our food sponsor Blue Buffalo, decided to stop helping rescues directly. This leaves rescues like Regal Dane in a desperate situation. We currently have over 110 Danes in rescue, and we have little food left (approx. 25 bags). We need your help with food donations.

    Empower Great Dane's Welfare

    Adopt

    Our Danes are spayed/neutered, current on vaccinations and microchipped prior to adoption. All adoptions are done within Arizona and New Mexico only at this time.

    MEET OUR DANES
    Donate

    All funds raised go towards the well-being of the Great Danes in our care. This includes their medical expenses, training requirements, spaying and neutering, and food

    SUPPORT OUR CAUSE
    Volunteer

    Volunteers help us by transporting Danes to medical appointments, helping at our events, delivering supplies to Fosters, and assisting us in doing home checks.
